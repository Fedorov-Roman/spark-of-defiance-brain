# Zone 2: Polar Oasis v2

## Theme
Frozen oasis with neon-lit trees. Crashed ship wreck. SUN outpost. Verticality.

## Size
~100×80 tiles. Heavy vertical emphasis.

## Mechanics Introduced
- Grapple (swing physics)
- Laser grids (disable via EMP or switches)
- Ice physics (slippery ground, reduced friction)

## Enemies
- Guard SUN (patrol)
- Elite Sniper (static tower, high damage)
- Drone Eye (more numerous)

## Boss: Helios-7
- **Arena:** Crashed ship hangar.
- **Mechanic:** EMP or shoot leg joints to paralyze → climb and hack core.
- **Phase 1:** Walks, stomps, fires missiles (dodge indicators).
- **Phase 2:** Legs damaged → switches to mortar mode (stay airborne with grapple).
- **Phase 3:** Core exposed. Time Dagger recommended for slow missiles.

## Secrets
- 3 Data Drives
- 1 Energy upgrade (Time Dagger duration +0.5s)
- 2 landing pads
