# Zone 1: Red Dunes

## Theme

Post-apocalyptic desert, rusted ruins, two alien moons.

## Mechanics

- **Dust storms**: Random 40-60s events. Visibility drops for ALL. Footsteps masked.
- **Verticality**: Sand dunes, cliff faces, ruins.
- **Secrets**: Hidden caves with data-drives.

## Enemies

- Drone Eye (flying patrol)
- Guard SUN (ground patrol)
- Silicon Beast (sound-hunter, rare)
