# Zone 3: Deep Labs v2

## Theme
Underground alien ruins. Gravity anomalies. Bioluminescent fungi. SUN experiments.

## Size
~140×70 tiles. Maze-like.

## Mechanics Introduced
- Gravity anomalies (walk on ceiling/walls)
- Time-locked doors (visible only during Time Dagger)
- Biomechanoid vulnerability system

## Enemies
- All previous types + corrupted experiments (fast, erratic)
- Security drones (tethered to charging stations)

## Boss: Biomechanoid
- **Arena:** Circular lab chamber.
- **Invincible without Time Dagger.**
- **With Time Dagger:** Weak points glow, attacks slow, can be damaged.
- **Phase 1:** Charge + slam. Dodge with dash.
- **Phase 2:** Spawns mini-drones. Use EMP.
- **Phase 3:** Gravity shifts (anomaly). Wall-run to avoid lasers.

## Secrets
- 5 Data Drives
- 1 secret ending lore (not alternate ending, just deep lore)
- 3 landing pads
