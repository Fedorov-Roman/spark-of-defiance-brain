# Zone 1: Red Dunes v2

## Theme
Endless rust-red sand dunes. Ancient metal ruins. Two small moons. Dust storms.

## Size
~120×60 tiles (3840×1920 px). 3 sub-areas.

## Mechanics Introduced
- Wall-slide, Wall-jump, Dash, Roll, Crouch, Ledge grab
- Stealth basics (grass, shadows, cones)
- Sniper rifle (1 ammo tutorial)
- Decoy (rock)

## Enemies
- Drone Eye (flying, waypoints)
- Guard SUN (ground patrol)
- Silicon Beast (sound-reactive, blind)

## Boss: Sand Leviathan
- **Arena:** Separate circular area (40×40 tiles).
- **Mechanic:** Shoot steam valves to release blinding steam → Leviathan exposes weak point → sniper or knife.
- **Phase 1:** Burrow → emerge → bite. Avoid by dash/roll.
- **Phase 2:** Summons sand waves (jump over).
- **Phase 3:** Steam valves only way to stun.

## Secrets
- 4 Data Drives
- 1 Grapple item (allows high exploration)
- 2 landing pads

## Environmental
- **Dust storms:** Random, 40–60 sec. Visibility 50%. Footstep noise masked. Both sides affected.
- **Ruins:** Cover, wall-run surfaces, hidden vents.
- **Sandfalls:** One-way drops (like Hollow Knight).
