# Guard SUN v2

## Stats
- HP: 2 (knife from behind = 1-hit; frontal = alert)
- Speed: 50 px/sec (patrol), 90 px/sec (alert)
- Vision: 90° cone, 120 px range
- Noise hearing: 80 px radius

## Behavior
- Ground patrol route (waypoints).
- Can investigate bodies (enters alert if body found).
- Melee attack: 1 heart damage. Wind-up 0.5 sec (dodgeable).

## Visual
- Humanoid in white/chrome armor, helmet with red visor.
- Carries baton/rifle (melee only for gameplay).

## Drops
- 20% chance: Ammo (+1 sniper round)
- 30% chance: Medkit
