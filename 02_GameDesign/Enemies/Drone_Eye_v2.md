# Drone Eye v2

## Stats
- HP: 1 (sniper or EMP kills)
- Speed: 40 px/sec (patrol), 80 px/sec (alert chase)
- Vision: 90° cone, 100 px range
- Noise hearing: 60 px radius

## Behavior
- Follows waypoints (circular or back-and-forth).
- On suspicious: moves to last noise, scans 3 sec.
- On alert: chases Kai, calls reinforcements after 5 sec.
- On lost: returns to patrol after 30 sec.

## Visual
- Floating sphere, single large blue camera lens, gold SUN emblem.
- Cone: semi-transparent blue (idle), yellow (suspicious), red (alert).

## Drops
- 10% chance: Battery (+30 energy)
- 50% chance: nothing
