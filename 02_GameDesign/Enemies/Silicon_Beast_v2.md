# Silicon Beast v2

## Stats
- HP: 3
- Speed: 70 px/sec (rush toward noise)
- Vision: None (blind)
- Noise hearing: 120 px radius (very sensitive)

## Behavior
- Patrols slowly (20 px/sec) until noise detected.
- Rushes toward noise source. If Kai is source: attacks.
- Cannot be stealth-killed (no back; armored).
- Weak to EMP (stuns for 5 sec).

## Visual
- Quadruped made of rusted metal and crystal. Glowing red seams.
- No eyes. Large audio sensors on head.

## Drops
- 50% chance: Data Drive
- 50% chance: Smoke bomb
