# Progression and Hub

## NPC Rescue System
Rescue → NPC appears in Hub → unlocks ability/shop.

| NPC | Zone | Unlock | Requirement |
|-----|------|--------|-------------|
| Uno (Engineer) | Z1 | Gadget shop | Rescue + 3 Data-Drives |
| Liara (Medic) | Z1 | HP upgrade, medkits | Rescue |
| Jaan (Blade Master) | Z2 | Air Dash, knife damage | Rescue |
| Elia (Chronomancer) | Z2 | Time Dagger, Echo | Rescue + 5 Data-Drives |
| Kess (Pilot) | Z3 | Fast Travel pads | Rescue |
| Finch (Comms) | Z3 | Full map, secret markers | Rescue + 8 Data-Drives |

## Data-Drives
- Collectible in world (hidden)
- Currency for NPC services
- 20 total in game
