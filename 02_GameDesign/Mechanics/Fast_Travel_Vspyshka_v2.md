# Fast Travel (Vspyshka) v2

## Unlock
- Rescue Kess (Pilot) in Zone 1.
- Before that: only Hub ↔ Zone 1 (story forced).

## Mechanics
- Interact with landing pad → open map screen.
- Select discovered pad → auto cutscene (3 sec) → load zone.
- During cutscene: Vspyshka sprite flies across starfield.

## Landing Pads
- 7–10 total across all zones + hub.
- Each pad discovered by walking near it.
- Hub pad is always active.

## Save on Travel
- Autosave before departure and after arrival.
- Player state (HP, ammo, gadgets) preserved.
- Zone state: enemies respawn on re-entry (per D15).
