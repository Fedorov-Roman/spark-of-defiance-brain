# Progression & Hub v2

## NPC Masters (6 total)
| NPC | Zone | Rescue Condition | Unlocks |
|-----|------|------------------|---------|
| **Uno** (Engineer) | Zone 1 | Reach workshop | Gadget shop (EMP, Smoke, Batteries) |
| **Liara** (Medic) | Zone 1 | Clear medical bay | +1 heart max, Medkits in hub |
| **Jaan** (Blade Master) | Zone 2 | Defeat mini-boss | Air Dash, Wall-jump upgrade |
| **Elia** (Chronomancer) | Zone 2 | Solve time puzzle | Time Dagger full power, Echo (invisibility after dash) |

## Hub "Spark Base"
- One scene. NPCs appear as `ProgressionManager` flags set.
- Each NPC has a "station" that visually upgrades when rescued.
- Uno shop: spend Data Drives for gadgets.
- Exit: Vspyshka terminal (fast travel to any discovered pad).

## Data Drives
- **Collectible:** 3–5 per zone (hidden).
- **Currency:** Used at Uno's shop and to activate some doors.
- **Lore:** Each drive contains audio log / document (cameo characters).

## Ability Unlock Order
1. Start: Wall-slide, Wall-jump, Dash (ground), Roll, Crouch, Ledge grab, Knife, Sniper (1 ammo).
2. Rescue Jaan: Air Dash (double jump equivalent).
3. Rescue Elia: Time Dagger (charged), Echo (invisible 0.5s after dash).
4. Rescue Kess: Fast travel, run speed +10%.
5. Rescue Liara: Max HP +1, hub medkits.
6. Rescue Uno: Gadgets (EMP, Smoke, Batteries).
7. Rescue Finch: Map, secret markers.

## Lock & Key
- **Dash gates:** Narrow passages (need Dash).
- **Grapple points:** High ledges (need Grapple — found as item in Zone 1).
- **EMP doors:** Electronic locks (need EMP or switch).
- **Time doors:** Only visible during Time Dagger slow.
