# Fast Travel (Vspyshka)

## Unlock
- Find Kess in Zone 3
- Fighter jet "Vspyshka" becomes available

## Mechanics
- Only from discovered landing pads (7-10 total)
- Automatic short cutscene
- Access via terminal in Hub

## Landing Pads
- Hub (default)
- Zone 1: Dunes Edge, Ruins Depths
- Zone 2: Oasis Rim, Lab Entrance
- Zone 3: Deep Shaft, Core Chamber
