# Combat and Weapons

## Energy Knife
- Stealth kill from behind (instant)
- Side kill if enemy stunned/paralyzed
- Frontal attack: 1 damage to Kai, enemy alerted

## Sniper Rifle "Whisper of Shadow"
- 5 rounds max, collectible in world
- Paralyzes target for 10s
- Disables electronics (drones)
- Silent (3m detection radius)
- Aim: mouse or right stick

## EMP Grenade
- Disables drones for 10s (fall to ground)
- 8s cooldown
