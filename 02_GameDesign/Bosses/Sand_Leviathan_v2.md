# Sand Leviathan v2

## Arena
Circular sand pit, 40×40 tiles. 4 steam valves on walls.

## Phases
1. **Burrow:** Submerges, sand ripples show position. Emerges under Kai (bite, 2 hearts). Dodge with dash/roll.
2. **Surface:** Exposed for 5 sec. Shoot steam valve → blinding steam → Leviathan stunned 3 sec → weak point (glowing throat) exposed.
3. **Repeat:** 3 stuns required.

## Attacks
- Bite: 2 hearts, close range.
- Tail whip: 1 heart, 180° arc.
- Sand wave: Pushback, must jump over.

## Visual
- Massive segmented worm, metallic armor plates, multiple blue eyes, rust-orange color.
- Scale: Kai is 1/20th its size.
