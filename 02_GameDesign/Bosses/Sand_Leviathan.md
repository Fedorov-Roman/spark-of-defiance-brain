# Sand Leviathan

## Profile

- **Type**: Colossal sand worm.
- **Arena**: Circular desert pit.
- **Mechanic**: Burrows, erupts, charges.
- **Weakness**: Steam valves (shoot to blind).

## Phases

1. **Phase 1**: Burrow → Erupt → Snap. Predictable patterns.
2. **Phase 2** (50% HP): Faster, adds sand spray.
3. **Phase 3** (25% HP): Desperate charge, steam valves critical.
