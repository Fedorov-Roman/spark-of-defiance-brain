# Biomechanoid v2

## Arena
Circular lab, 30×30 tiles. Gravity shifts during Phase 3.

## Core Mechanic
**Invincible without Time Dagger.**
With Time Dagger: weak points glow cyan, attacks slowed, damageable.

## Phases
1. **Charge & Slam:** Rushes Kai. Dodge sideways. Slam creates shockwave (jump).
2. **Drone swarm:** Spawns 6 mini-drones. EMP clears them instantly.
3. **Gravity anomaly:** `up_direction` shifts every 10 sec. Wall-run to avoid horizontal laser sweeps.

## Weak Points
- Chest core (exposed during Time Dagger).
- Back vents (vulnerable after slam recovery).

## Visual
- Humanoid, 4 tiles tall. Exposed muscles + metal endoskeleton. Glowing cyan veins. Faceless.
