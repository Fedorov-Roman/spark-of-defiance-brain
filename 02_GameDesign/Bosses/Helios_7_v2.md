# Helios-7 v2

## Arena
Crashed ship hangar. 50×30 tiles. Elevated platforms.

## Phases
1. **Walking:** Stomps (shockwave, dodge jump). Fires missiles (3, homing, destroyable with sniper).
2. **Damaged legs:** Collapses to mortar mode. Shells rain from above. Use grapple to stay airborne.
3. **Core exposed:** Climb back, hack terminal (3 sec channel). Time Dagger slows missiles for safety.

## Weakness
- Leg joints: paralyze with sniper (10 sec) → climb.
- EMP: disables missile launchers for 8 sec.

## Visual
- 4-legged mech, 10 tiles tall. Solar panel "mane". Yellow SUN markings. Glowing red core.
