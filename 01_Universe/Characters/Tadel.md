# Tadel
**Role:** Mentor (voice only / audio logs)  
**Faction:** UFO (Spark Cell leader)  
**Status:** MIA / presumed dead

## Background
Veteran infiltrator. Lost an arm on Caldea (replaced with prosthetic). Founded Spark Cell on Postulate. Trained Kai from age 14.

## Audio Logs
Found in Data Drives across all zones. Tadel gives mission context, warns about SUN experiments, expresses pride in Kai.

## Fate
Captured by SUN during raid on Zone 2. Fate unknown by end of game.
