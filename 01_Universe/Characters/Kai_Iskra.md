# Kai "Iskra"
**Role:** Protagonist (Player)  
**Age:** 22  
**Origin:** Postulate, Red Dunes sector  
**Faction:** UFO (Spark Cell)

## Backstory
Family killed when SUN atmospheric processors caused a dust storm beyond safety parameters. Kai survived in a buried shelter for 3 days before UFO scouts found him. Trained by cell leader Tadel (mentioned in audio logs only).

## Personality
Silent protagonist. Expresses through actions, not words. Determined, protective of innocents, haunted by survivor guilt.

## Appearance
- Hooded dark tactical cloak
- Red scarf covering lower face (breath filter)
- Glowing cyan energy dagger ("Time Dagger" prototype)
- Compact sniper rifle on back
- Desert-worn boots with magnetic grips

## Abilities
- Wall-slide / Wall-jump (natural agility)
- Dash (cell training)
- Time Dagger (Elia's training, incomplete at start)
- Knife stealth takedowns

## Cameo in Lore
Audio logs in Data Drives reference Kai as "the spark that survived the storm."
