# UN (Unified Navigation)
**Motto:** "Every step toward the goal"  
**Role:** Neutral navigation monopoly

## Tech
- Quantum Repeater Hubs: orbital stations with NV-center diamond quantum processors
- Stable Entanglement Network (SEN): instant hub-to-hub data
- Relativistic trajectory calculation: required for safe hyperspace jumps

## Neutrality
Does not take sides. Sells navigation to all factions. Without UN, interstellar travel is blind and deadly. Stations are sterile, minimalist, crystal-like.

## On Postulate
One hub in orbit. SUN has embargoed Postulate's civilian access; Kai cannot leave planet without UN clearance (plot reason for grounded Vspyshka until Kess rescued).
