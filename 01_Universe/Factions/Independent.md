# Independent
**Motto:** "Free market — free man!"  
**Role:** Libertarian enclaves

## Ideology
- Minimal state, maximum private initiative
- Mercenary courts, hired armies
- Sacred private property
- Anti-SUN (taxes) and anti-UFO (collectivism)

## Status
Some smugglers on Postulate sell goods to both sides. Not a major faction in game.
