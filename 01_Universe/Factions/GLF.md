# GLF (Galactic League of the Free)
**Motto:** "Every race under its own sun!"  
**Role:** Xenophobic isolationists

## Ideology
- Racial purity zones
- Ban on interspecies migration
- Return to "ancestral territories"
- Anti-hybridization

## Status
Hated by both SUN and UFO. Marginal. Some presence on Postulate among displaced colonists. Mentioned in lore as fringe radicals.
