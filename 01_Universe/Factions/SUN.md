# SUN (Sovereign United Nexus)
**Motto:** "Peace under Our Sun"  
**Government:** Corporate oligarchy disguised as democratic council.

## Ideology
- Sovereign Supremacy: only SUN can maintain galactic order.
- Necessary Stability: any change is terrorism.
- Condemnation of Separatist Violence: freedom fighters = terrorists.
- Lawful Suppression: SUN wars are "peacekeeping."

## Symbols
- Trident star in tech circle
- Cold blue metallic, coal-black, silver-white
- Clean smooth lines, hidden panels, blue-white glows

## Military
- NIF (Nexus Imperial Fleet): Sun Hammer destroyers, Orbital Suppressors
- Solar Wardens: shock troops in white chrome armor
- GBR: internal security / anti-terror
- GNN: propaganda network with mind-control tech

## On Postulate
Occupies polar regions. Runs secret labs in ancient ruins. Uses local silicon fauna for bio-weapon research. Dismisses native population as "non-citizens."
