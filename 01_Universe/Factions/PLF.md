# PLF (People's Liberation Front)
**Motto:** "When there's nothing to say, say less than more"  
**Role:** Reformist splinter from UFO

## Ideology
- Evolutionary reform, not revolution
- Alliance with "progressive bourgeoisie"
- Legal methods: strikes, unions, elections
- Criticizes UFO: "Your radicalism pushes masses away"

## Status
Recognized by SUN as "workers' representatives" (controlled opposition). Kai's cell rejects them. Mentioned in Data Drives as cautionary tale.
