# UFO (United Front of Objection)
**Motto:** "From Spark to Blaze!"  
**Government:** Decentralized Concord Network. No central leader.

## Ideology
- Collective ownership of production means
- Direct democracy via local Concords
- Scientific progress for all, not weapons
- Solidarity: aid to besieged worlds

## Symbols
- Stylized flame/flash under horse (in shield or rounded rect)
- Bright red, coal-black, silver metallic
- Speed, tech, passion, welded seams aesthetic

## Military
- Blades: fast mobile strike groups (Flash-class fighters)
- Sparks: partisans, saboteurs, strikers
- Circle Seers: scientists, hackers, engineers
- Living Nets: underground comms & AI assistants

## On Postulate
Spark Cell operates from hidden base in ruins. Kai is their operative. Cell lacks heavy weapons; relies on infiltration and sabotage.
