# Lore Bible
*Universe: Kill a Horse of Freedom. Year 4717.*

## Timeline
- **4191:** SUN (Sovereign United Nexus) formed by corporate oligarchies.
- **4338:** BEC-38 (Great Economic Crisis). SUN establishes non-interference pact with developing worlds.
- **4717:** Present. Postulate is a SUN-occupied planet used for secret weapons research.

## Key Factions
- [[SUN]] — Corporate-military hegemony. "Order under Our Sun."
- [[UFO]] — United Front of Objection. Resistance network. "From Spark to Blaze."
- [[UN]] — Unified Navigation. Monopoly on FTL navigation tech. Neutral.
- [[PLF]] — People's Liberation Front. Reformist wing of UFO. Rejected by Kai's cell.
- [[GLF]] — Galactic League of the Free. Xenophobic isolationists.
- [[Independent]] — Libertarian free-marketeers.

## Postulate
Desert planet. Surface: extreme iron-oxide deserts. Subsurface: ancient alien ruins used by SUN labs. Climate: dust storms, extreme day/night temperature shift. Life: silicon-based extremophiles.

## Kai "Iskra"
Native Postulate survivor. Family killed in SUN dust-storm negligence (terraforming accident). Rescued by UFO cell. Silent. Skilled in infiltration, acrobatics, time-manipulation (Chronomancer training incomplete). Wears hooded cloak, red scarf, energy dagger.
