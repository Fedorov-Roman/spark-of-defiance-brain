# MOC: Planets

## Game World

- [[Postulate]] — Red desert, ancient ruins, secret labs. Setting of *Spark of Defiance*.

## Other Worlds (Lore)

- [[Silvana]] — Mega-forest, 300m trees, high O2.
- [[Caldea]] — Hyper-volcanic, silicon life, kunduki clans.
- [[Ferrum]] — Heavy metal world, no flora, silicon drillers.
- [[Efi]] — 0.4G, sky whales, roxellana nomads.
- [[Kios]] — Ice prison, -80°C, subglacial oceans.
- [[Oil]] — 27-state confederation, industrial powerhouse.
