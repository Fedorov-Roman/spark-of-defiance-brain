# Postulate
**Type:** Desert / Ruins  
**Atmosphere:** Thin, oxygen-poor. Dust storms.  
**Gravity:** 0.9G  
**Day/Night:** 28-hour day, extreme temperature swing.

## Surface
Red iron-oxide dunes. Ancient metal ruins (pre-human civilization). Toxic salt flats. Deep canyons with hidden water.

## Fauna
- Silicon Burrowers: six-legged, diamond-tipped drills. Feed on minerals.
- Sand Worms (Leviathan): apex predator. Hibernates for decades.
- Dust Mites: microscopic, cause equipment failure.

## Flora
None visible. Subsurface: extremophile algae in canyon seeps.

## Human Presence
- SUN: Polar research bases, orbital stations, secret labs.
- Natives: displaced to canyon edges. Live in scrap-metal shanties.
- UFO: Hidden cell in ruins.

## Zones in Game
1. Red Dunes (surface)
2. Polar Oasis (SUN outpost zone)
3. Deep Labs (subsurface ruins)
